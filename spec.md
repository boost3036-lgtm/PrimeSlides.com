# PrimeSlides - PPT making company website

## overview
A modern, professional, mobile-friendlywebsite for PrimeSlides, a PowerPoint Presentation making company. The website showcases the company's services, portfolio and providescontact information for potential clients. The brand emphasizes presenting with confidence through professional presentation services.

## Brand Identity
- company name: **PrimeSlides**
- tagline: **"present with confidence"**
- The tagline should appear beneath the logo or hero heading on the Home page
- All metadata (title tags, descriptions, social previews) should reflect the PrimeSlides brand and tagline consistently

## Pages and Content

### Home Page
- Hero section with catchy headline about professional PPT services
- PrimeSlides branding with "present with confidence" tagline prominently displayed
- Brief company introduction
- Key service highlights
- Call-to-action button directing to services or contact page

### About Us Page
- PrimeSlides company story and mission
- Team information or company values
- Professional background and expertise
- Call-to-action to view services or get in touch

### Services Page
- List of PPT services offered by PrimeSlides (e.g., business presentations, pitch decks, training materials)
- Brief description of each service
- Benefits of choosing PrimeSlides
- Call-to-action to view pricing or contact

### Portfolio Page
- Showcase of previous work examples by PrimeSlides
- Different categories of presentations created
- Visual examples or case studies
- Call-to-action to discuss a project

### Pricing Page
- PrimeSlides service packages or pricing tiers
- What's included in each package
- Clear pricing structure
- Call-to-action to get started or contact for custom quote

### Contact Us Page
- Contact form with fields for name, email, and message
- PrimeSlides contact information (address, phone, email)
- Business hours
- Call-to-action to submit the form

###  Technical Features
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
- Navigation menu with mobile toggle functionality
- Smooth scrolling between sections
- Interactive CTA buttons
- Form validation for contact form
- Clean, colorful-but-professional design aesthetic
- Consistent PrimeSlides branding across all components and responsive views

### Backend Requirements
- Store contact form submissions with name, email, and message
- Provide endpoint to retrieve submitted contact forms for company review

### Frontend Features
- Mobile-responsive navigation with PrimeSlides branding
- Interactive elements and hover effects
- Form submission handling
- Smooth page transitions and scrolling
- Professional color scheme and typography
- Consistent brand name and tagline display across all pages
- Updated metadata and social preview information reflecting PrimeSlides brand
